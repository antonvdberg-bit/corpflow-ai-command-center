Auth users (factory only)

Passwords are never visible. They are stored as one-way hashes (like banks do). This page lists who can log in and lets you set a new password (generated or typed). Use MASTER_ADMIN_KEY or log in as factory admin on this browser first.

Why clients cannot live with “DIY auth” forever: if we lose a day on login internally, clients will too — unless we ship managed sign-in (e.g. Clerk, Auth0, Supabase Auth, Google Workspace) and keep CorpFlow for tickets and data. This tool is the break-glass ops console until then.
