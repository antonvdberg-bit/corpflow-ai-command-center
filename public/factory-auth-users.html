Staff logins — who can sign in?

Use this page after you add a client workspace. It lists email accounts (username + password hash). Passwords are never shown again — only reset or generate new. Paste MASTER_ADMIN_KEY below if you are not already signed in as Factory Admin on this same hostname (e.g. core.corpflowai.com).

Finding someone:
  • Login email goes in Email / username — not in tenant id.
  • Tenant id is the short workspace id (e.g. corpflowai, luxe-maurice) — not an email address.
  • Leave both blank and click Load list to see recent accounts (up to 500).
Long term: replace DIY email/password with managed sign-in (Clerk, Auth0, etc.) for clients. This screen is the operator confirmation tool until then.
