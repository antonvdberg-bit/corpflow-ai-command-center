---

## 1. What this pilot is

Living Word is testing a new way to **keep member contact information accurate and up to date**, using a system operated by CorpFlow on the church’s behalf.

- The pilot involves a **small group of about 10 adult volunteers**, chosen by the church.
- It is **voluntary**. You only take part if you agree.
- The goal is to test the process before deciding whether to use it more widely.
- **Children and young people are not included** in this pilot.

---

## 2. What information we collect (pilot v1)

If you take part, we may collect and store:

- Your **name**
- Your **email address**
- Your **WhatsApp / mobile number**
- Your **preferred way to be contacted** (for example: email, WhatsApp, or phone call)
- **Member / contact details needed for church administration** (for example, keeping the church directory current)
- **Update confirmation details** — the date/time you confirmed your information and that you gave consent

## 3. What we do NOT collect in this pilot

To keep the pilot simple and low-risk, we will **not** collect in pilot v1:

- **Children’s or youth personal details**
- **Prayer requests or counselling notes** (no sensitive personal/spiritual free-text)
- **Medical, legal, or financial details**

If the church later wants to collect any of the above, that would require a **separate decision and a separate, stronger approval and privacy process**.

---

## 4. Why we collect it (purpose)

We use this information only to:

1. **Verify and update your church contact information** so records are accurate.
2. **Test the CorpFlow member-management workflow** (does the update-and-review process work well?).
3. **Reduce the admin load on volunteers** who currently update records by hand.

We will **not** use your pilot information for marketing, advertising, or anything unrelated to church administration.

---

## 5. Where your information is stored

- During the pilot, your information is stored in a **CorpFlow-managed environment**, **subject to church board approval**.
- The **final production storage location is still under review** (this may include a Mauritius-based or other approved option). The board will decide the long-term storage location before any wider rollout.
- We will keep the list of service providers involved (for example, hosting and database providers) available for the church on request.

---

## 6. Who can see your information

- Only **authorised church administrators and authorised CorpFlow operators** can access pilot information.
- Access is limited to people who need it to run the pilot and update records.
- Your information is **not** shared with the public, with other church members, or with the church Business Network directory as part of this pilot.

---

## 7. How long we keep it (retention)

- Pilot information is kept **only as long as needed to run and evaluate the pilot**.
- If the church **does not** proceed to production, pilot information will be **deleted or returned** as the board directs.
- Pilot information will only be **kept/migrated into a production system** if the board **approves** moving to production.

---

## 8. Your rights as a volunteer

You are in control. You may:

- **Choose not to take part** (participation is voluntary).
- **Ask us to correct** any information that is wrong.
- **Ask us to remove you** from the pilot and delete your pilot information.
- **Opt out at any time**, without needing to give a reason.

To do any of the above, contact the **data contact person** named below.
